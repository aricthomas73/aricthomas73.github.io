About

What is Step Journey Tracker?

Step Journey Tracker connects to Apple HealthKit to display your daily step counts, celebrate milestones with trophies, and let you personalize your experience with themes. Home screen widgets keep your progress visible throughout the day, and a Pro upgrade unlocks the full feature set.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Make sure you've granted HealthKit access. Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Step Journey and enable Steps read access. Then relaunch the app.
Widgets refresh on iOS's own schedule. Try removing and re-adding the widget, or long-press the widget and tap "Edit Widget" to trigger a refresh. Make sure Background App Refresh is enabled for Step Journey in iPhone Settings.
Trophies are awarded automatically based on your step history in HealthKit. They're calculated from your actual historical data, so past achievements will be recognized even on a fresh install.
Pro unlocks all app themes, full trophy history, extended statistics, and all widget styles. It's a one-time purchase — no subscriptions.
Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app settings or on the upgrade screen. Ensure you are signed into the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase.
Step Journey reads from HealthKit rather than tracking GPS or running background processes continuously. Battery impact should be minimal. Widgets use iOS's standard background refresh mechanism.
