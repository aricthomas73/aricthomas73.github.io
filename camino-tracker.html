About

What is Camino Tracker?

Camino Tracker is your personal guide to the Camino de Santiago routes. View detailed route maps, track which stages you've walked, mark sections as completed or in progress, and plan future legs of your journey — whether you're preparing for your first Camino or logging a return trip.
