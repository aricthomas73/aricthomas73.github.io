About

What is Retire Countdown?

Retire Countdown helps you track your journey to retirement with a live countdown timer. Built for pension-based retirement planning, the app lets you enter your service details and retirement targets, estimate your monthly pension based on years of service and salary, track milestones, and add a countdown widget to your home screen so your retirement date is always front and center.
