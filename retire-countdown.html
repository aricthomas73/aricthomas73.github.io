Retire Countdown is designed for pension-based retirement planning. Enter your service details and retirement targets, and the app handles the rest. We plan to expand support for additional pension systems in future updates.
The estimates are based on publicly available pension formulas and the information you provide (years of service, salary, retirement age). They are intended to give you a general picture and should not be used as official financial advice. Always verify with your pension system's official resources.
